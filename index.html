FAQ

How do I get to Villa Honegg?

The easiest way to reach Villa Honegg is by car.

  • Zurich Airport: approximately 1 hour by car
  • Basel Airport: approximately 1 hour 30 minutes by car

Taxi, private transfer and rental car services are available from both airports.

Is parking available?

Yes. Parking is available for guests at Villa Honegg.

What is the dress code?

Saturday evening welcome aperitif and barbecue: Smart Casual.

Sunday ceremony, reception and evening celebrations: Formal Attire.

Can I arrive before check-in?

Rooms will be available from 15:00 on Saturday, 5 September 2026. Guests arriving earlier are welcome to enjoy the hotel grounds and common areas.

What facilities are available at Villa Honegg?

Guests can enjoy the infinity pool, spa & wellness area, fitness centre, restaurant & bar, panoramic lake and mountain views, and beautiful outdoor terraces.

For additional information and facilities, please visit the official Villa Honegg website.

What time is checkout?

Checkout is at 12:00 on Monday, 7 September 2026. Food and refreshments will still be available until 14:00.
